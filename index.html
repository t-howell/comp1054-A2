The History

The gummy bear was first invented by the German Hans Riegel Sr. after seeing trained bears performing across Europe. He crafted a gummy treat using gum arabic in 1922 which was larger than the gummy bears we have today - and called it the Dancing Bear. Those bears were also fruity in flavour.
